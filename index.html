I. Foundational Excellence

Auditing Accounts & Fortifying Core Infrastructure

Step 1: Interactive Cohort Analysis Dashboard

The primary challenge is an unsustainable LTV:CAC ratio. To fix this, we must move beyond blended averages and analyze performance by specific user segments. A granular cohort analysis framework is necessary to provide a clear understanding of user quality and profitability based on where, when, and how they were acquired.

Key Dimensions for Segmentation
  • Acquisition Channel: Meta, Google, TikTok, Organic.
  • Campaign: e.g., `Meta_Prospecting_Interest_Restaurants`, `Google_PMax_HighValue`.
  • Creative Angle: e.g., `ValueProp_50%Off`, `Discovery_HiddenGems`, `UGC_Testimonial`.
  • Geography: City-level analysis to identify regional performance variations.
Key Metrics to Track per Cohort
  • Cost per Install (CPI) & Cost per Trial (CPT): Top-of-funnel efficiency metrics.
  • Trial-to-Paid Conversion Rate (CVR): The most critical lagging indicator, measured at Day 30 post-trial start.
  • Referral Rate: The percentage of users in a cohort who successfully refer at least one new user.
  • Blended LTV (D30, D90, D365): The cumulative revenue generated per user over time, incorporating subscription revenue and a modeled value for referrals.

The fact that referral activity boosts LTV but is unmeasured is a significant blind spot. Campaigns acquiring valuable "promoters" might be prematurely deactivated. Prioritizing a methodology to assign a monetary value to each referral is essential for an accurate assessment of performance.

This interactive dashboard demonstrates how to isolate performance and identify which acquisition strategies bring in the most valuable users.

The LTV chart would be accompanied by charts comparing Trial-to-Paid CVR and Referral Rate by acquisition channel to provide a multi-dimensional view of user quality.

Step 2: Attribution & Measurement

A) Multi-Touch Attribution Model Recommendation

The current last-click model undervalues crucial upper-funnel touchpoints. To get an accurate view of the customer journey, I recommend a phased transition to a multi-touch attribution model. This interactive visual demonstrates the strategic benefit of this shift.

Short-Term Recommendation: Position-Based

Why? It's an immediate improvement that's readily available in ad platforms. It instantly begins to properly value the first and last touchpoints, providing a more balanced view than last-click without requiring complex setup.

Long-Term Recommendation: Data-Driven

Why? It's the industry gold standard for accuracy. It uses machine learning to assign credit based on true impact, but requires significant conversion data and technical setup (via an MMP like Adjust) to work effectively.

B) Predictive Conversion Model

To shorten the 1-month optimization feedback loop, we'll build a model to predict paid subscriptions based on the first 7 days of a trial. This allows for near real-time optimization based on predicted revenue.

How it Works:

The model will use key behavioral signals to generate a "Predicted LTV" score.

  • Input Signals: App open frequency, first deal redemption, viewing the subscription page, trial cancellation.
  • Implementation: The "Predicted LTV" score is sent to ad platforms via Adjust postbacks, enabling value-based bidding.

Step 3: Deep-Funnel Targeting & Bidding

Audience Creation Approach
High-Value Lookalikes (LALs):
  • LAL 1% of Annual Subscribers
  • LAL 1% of Top 10% Referrers
  • LAL 1% of Users with Predicted LTV > €50
High-Intent Custom Audiences:
  • Retargeting: Engaged trial users who haven't redeemed their first deal.
  • Exclusion: Users with low engagement and a high risk of churning.
Bidding Strategy Recommendation
Which Strategy: Value-Based Bidding

I would choose Target ROAS (tROAS). Why? Because it directly aligns the ad platforms' algorithms with our business goal of profitability. It bids for users most likely to generate high long-term revenue, not just the cheapest trial.

Implementation Rules:
  • Ensure campaigns have >50 conversions/week before activating.
  • Test on 1-2 high-performing campaigns first to set benchmarks.
  • Start with a conservative tROAS target and increase it as performance stabilizes.

Step 4: Discrepancy Investigation & Data Needs

Hypothesis Framework for Causes
  • Attribution Window Mismatch: Platforms using different lookback windows (e.g., 7-day click vs. 1-day click).
  • Click vs. View-Through: Ad platforms reporting view-through conversions not credited by the MMP.
  • Data Lag & SKAdNetwork: Delays and modeling in iOS 14+ data causing timing mismatches.
  • Technical Errors: Faulty SDK implementation or broken server postbacks.
Action Plan for Data Team
  • Audit Adjust ➔ Ads Setup: Verify postback configurations and event mapping for all key funnel events (Install, Trial, Subscription, Referral).
  • Prioritize Server-Side Tracking: Expedite implementation of Meta CAPI & TikTok Events API to mitigate data loss and improve event matching.
  • Enhance BI / GA4 Reporting: Build a unified dashboard pulling cost data from ads platforms and conversion data from Adjust (as the source of truth). This dashboard must visualize LTV:CAC by cohort and the full user acquisition funnel.

II. Disciplined Expansion

Omnichannel Go-To-Market Strategy for Scotland

Market & Audience Deep Dive: The "Savvy Foodie"

Success in Scotland requires a hyper-localized approach, targeting a specific persona and navigating a competitive landscape.

Target Persona: The Savvy Foodie (18-35)

Young professionals and students in Edinburgh and Glasgow who are experience-seekers, influenced by social media, and value-conscious. They seek high-quality dining at accessible prices and strongly prefer to support local Scottish businesses.

Competitive Landscape

Direct competitors include `tastecard` and `First Table`. Indirect competitors are booking platforms like `TheFork`. NeoTaste's unique position is the combination of curated discovery of trendy local spots with exclusive deals. Our messaging must champion this duality and our support for local restaurants.
